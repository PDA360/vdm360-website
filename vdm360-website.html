The Framework

Value-Driven Management 360° (VDM 360°) is a holistic, value-oriented approach that aims to navigate companies through the challenges of the modern business and market environment. VDM 360° integrates proven management theories and practices with innovative methods to address complex, multidimensional, and digital challenges.

This philosophy finds application especially in the areas of Growth, Transformation, Turnaround, and Crisis Management. The framework operates across all enterprise levels simultaneously — strategic, tactical, and operative — ensuring that long-term intent and short-term execution remain permanently aligned.

In addition to these four focus areas, VDM 360° offers five forms of collaboration to tailor services individually to the needs of each company. By leveraging these forms of collaboration, VDM 360° provides comprehensive and customized solutions for businesses in the AEC industry worldwide.

Four Focus Areas

Growth
Corporate Leadership & Strategy · Business Development & Expansion · Customer Focus & Agile Sales

Within VDM 360°, Growth plays a central role. This area focuses on developing and implementing sustainable growth strategies to make companies future-proof and competitive. Corporate leadership, business development, and customer-oriented sales approaches are at the center.

Corporate Leadership & StrategyDevelopment and implementation of long-term strategies aimed at sustainable growth. Creation of a clear vision and mission that inspires and guides the entire company.

Business Development & ExpansionIdentification and development of new business opportunities and markets. Building strategic partnerships and alliances to expand market reach.

Customer Focus & Agile SalesImplementation of customer-oriented strategies that place customer needs at the center. Use of agile sales approaches to respond quickly to market changes and customer feedback.

Transformation
Performance Improvement · Business Intelligence & Data Analytics · Digital Transformation

Within VDM 360°, Transformation is a decisive area. This section concentrates on transforming companies through performance improvements, data-driven decisions, and digital innovations — with the goal of increasing efficiency, creating new value opportunities, and fostering an agile, innovation-driven corporate culture.

Performance ImprovementOptimizing operational efficiency and effectiveness through process improvements and innovation initiatives. Application of Lean and Six Sigma methods to reduce waste and improve quality.

Business Intelligence & Data AnalyticsUse of advanced data analysis and BI tools to make informed business decisions. Implementation of data strategies that enable real-time insights and forecasts.

Digital TransformationIntegration of digital technologies across all business areas to create new value opportunities. Fostering a digital culture that supports innovation and agility.

Turnaround
Change Management · Restructuring · (Distressed) Mergers & Acquisitions

Within VDM 360°, Turnaround is an essential area. This section concentrates on getting companies back on course through effective change management, restructuring, and strategic mergers and acquisitions — with the goal of ensuring financial stability and increasing profitability.

Change ManagementConducting effective change management processes to guide organizations through transformation. Ensuring employee participation and acceptance through clear communication and training.

RestructuringRedesigning business structures and processes to ensure financial stability and efficiency. Rationalization of operations and resource allocation to increase profitability.

(Distressed) Mergers & AcquisitionsConducting and integrating mergers and acquisitions, especially in financially distressed situations. Assessment and mitigation of risks associated with corporate takeovers.

Crises
Risk & Crisis Management · Capital Access & Liquidity · Restructuring & Insolvency

Within VDM 360°, Crisis Management is an essential area. This section concentrates on stabilizing companies through effective risk management, securing capital access, and targeted restructuring measures — with the goal of keeping companies capable of action in difficult times and ensuring orderly resolution in the event of insolvency.

Risk & Crisis ManagementIdentification and assessment of potential risks that could threaten the company. Development and implementation of crisis management plans to respond effectively to unexpected events.

Capital Access & LiquidityEnsuring access to necessary capital and liquidity to maintain business operations. Negotiations with investors, banks, and other financial institutions for support in times of crisis.

Restructuring & InsolvencyDevelopment and implementation of restructuring plans to restore financial health. Conducting insolvency proceedings, if necessary, to ensure orderly resolution and best possible creditor protection.

Five Forms of Collaboration

01
Institute
Enhance Your Knowledge. Enrich Your Business.
The VDM 360° Institute offers specialized articles, training, and development programs aimed at expanding the knowledge and skills of participants. Our holistic approach to knowledge transfer promotes comprehensive 360° value creation that helps companies successfully master the challenges of the modern business and market environment. Our tailored programs are designed to deepen professional expertise and improve practical skills — providing current and relevant content to ensure participants are best prepared for dynamic market requirements. Our experts offer in-depth knowledge and many years of experience to meet the individual needs of each company.
02
Advisory
Optimize Your Strategy. Develop Your Company.
The VDM 360° Advisory offering supports companies in optimizing their strategies and efficiently achieving their business goals. Our holistic, value-oriented approach combines proven management methods with innovative solutions to support companies in overcoming complex, multidimensional, and digital challenges. Our tailored advisory services are designed to help improve strategies and strengthen market position. Through sound analysis and practice-oriented recommendations, we ensure that companies can make the best decisions. With a focus on strategic development and sustainable value creation, we support companies in effectively achieving their business goals — promoting a comprehensive view that considers all aspects of the value chain.
03
Interim
Bridge Gaps. Lead to Optimal Performance.
Bridge gaps in your organization with experienced interim managers and lead the company to optimal performance. Whether due to sudden departures, mergers, takeovers, or restructuring — our interim managers ensure smooth operations. With a focus on strategic implementation and operational efficiency, our interim managers help your company achieve comprehensive value creation. They work closely with your team to overcome immediate challenges and implement sustainable solutions. Through our interim management services, companies gain valuable insights and tools to improve processes and increase performance. Our interim managers identify inefficiencies, drive innovations forward, and foster a culture of continuous improvement.
04
Board
Lead with Vision. Secure Long-Term Success.
Lead with vision through strategic initiatives at the supervisory level and ensure effective governance and long-term success. Our board services provide companies with experienced board members who have in-depth industry knowledge and extensive experience in corporate governance. They support the supervisory board in fulfilling its control and advisory function and help develop and implement strategic initiatives. Our board members bring fresh perspectives and innovative ideas that help companies respond to the challenges of the modern business world. They work closely with management to develop long-term strategies and ensure that the company's goals are achieved sustainably.
05
Equity
Maximize Investment Potential. Foster Financial Growth.
Maximize investment potential with tailor-made equity solutions that promote financial growth and stability. Our equity services provide companies with access to the capital they need to realize their growth plans and optimize their financial structure. We work closely with investors and companies to develop customized solutions that meet the specific needs of each situation. Our equity solutions are designed to maximize the return on investment while minimizing risk. We offer a comprehensive range of services — from equity financing to exit strategies — to ensure that companies achieve their financial goals and create sustainable value.
